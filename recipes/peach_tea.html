Homemade peach Tea recipe

Description

This delicious iced peach tea tastes eerily similar to a famous Italian chain restaurant's version. Serve over ice and garnish with peach slices, if desired.

ingredients

Peach Syrup:

  • 1 cups water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 slices fresh peach, or more to taste

Tea

  • 6 cups water
  • 3 black tea bags

Steps

  1. Make syrup: Mix together water, sugar, and peach slices in a small pot; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer, crushing peach slices as you stir, until sugar is dissolved, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat, cover, and allow peaches to infuse syrup, about 30 minutes. Strain to remove peach slices.
  2. Make tea: Bring water to a boil in a medium pot. Remove from heat and add tea bags; cover and steep for about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove tea bags from the pot and pour in syrup. Cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until ready to serve.
